Farm-to-Restaurant Ingredient Sourcing

Bread

Used in: Pane e Marmellata, Bruschetta al Pomodoro

From Willow Springs Mill: Stone-ground organic wheat milled and baked daily to ensure fresh loaves.

Butter

Used in: Pane e Marmellata, Cornetto, Amaretti Cookies

From Green Pastures Dairy: Churned from cream of pasture-raised cows without hormones, salted in small batches.

Fruit Preserves

Used in: Pane e Marmellata, Crespelle Dolci

Seasonal berries from Blueberry Hill Farm, hand-picked and slow-cooked with minimal sugar.

Flour

Used in: Vegan Pancakes, Cornetto, Crespelle Dolci, Four Cheese Ravioli, Pizza Margherita, Tiramisu, Pastry Shells

Red Mill Organic Flour: Milled locally from rotational wheat crops promoting soil health.

Baking Powder

Used in: Vegan Pancakes

From PureRise Pantry: Aluminum-free mix sourced regionally for clean leavening.

Plant Milk

Used in: Vegan Pancakes

Almond & Oat blend from NutriFarm Co: Crafted from local almonds and oats, no added stabilizers.

Fresh Berries

Used in: Vegan Pancakes

Morning Berry Orchard: Picked at dawn to lock in flavor before delivery.

Maple Syrup

Used in: Vegan Pancakes

Wildwood Maple: Sustainably tapped Vermont syrup, pure and unblended.

Yeast

Used in: Cornetto, Pizza Margherita

Fresh baker's yeast from ActiveRise Bakers: Cultivated and packaged locally for peak activity.

Jam

Used in: Cornetto, Crespelle Dolci

House-made strawberry and apricot jams from seasonal fruit, cooked in small batches.

Cream

Used in: Cornetto, Tiramisu

From Green Pastures Dairy: Farm-fresh cream churned and whipped on-site.

Nutella

Used in: Cornetto

Imported and used sparingly to complement house bakery flavors.

Espresso

Used in: Cappuccino & Biscotti, Tiramisu

Roasted by Sunrise Coffee Roasters from single-origin beans, brewed fresh per order.

Milk

Used in: Cappuccino & Biscotti, Crespelle Dolci

Whole milk from Green Pastures Dairy: Pasteurized and delivered daily.

Almond Biscotti

Used in: Cappuccino & Biscotti

Baked daily using local almonds, durum wheat, and minimal sugar.

Eggs

Used in: Frittata di Verdure, Crespelle Dolci, Four Cheese Ravioli, Amaretti Cookies, Tiramisu, Pastry Shells

Free Range Farms: Pasture-raised hens, fed non-GMO feed.

Zucchini

Used in: Frittata di Verdure

Valley Produce Co: Harvested the same morning for optimal crunch.

Peppers

Used in: Frittata di Verdure

Valley Produce Co: Organic bell peppers in seasonal colors.

Onions

Used in: Frittata di Verdure, Minestrone Soup, Insalata di Finocchi e Arance

Valley Produce Co: Sweet onions harvested in early morning dew.

Ricotta

Used in: Ricotta & Honey Crostini, Crespelle Dolci, Four Cheese Ravioli, Ricotta Cheesecake, Cannoli

Honey

Used in: Ricotta & Honey Crostini, Yogurt con Miele e Noci

Wildflower blend from Meadow Bee Apiary: Sustainably harvested local honey.

Walnuts

Used in: Yogurt con Miele e Noci

Orchard Fresh Walnuts: Hand-cracked and lightly toasted in-house.

Yogurt

Used in: Yogurt con Miele e Noci

From Green Pastures Dairy: Creamy yogurt cultured traditionally with live cultures.

Arborio Rice

Wild Mushrooms

Truffle Oil

Vegan Mozzarella
